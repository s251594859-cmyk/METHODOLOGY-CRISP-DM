CASE STUDY

Development a Website of Data Science Methodology

SCENARIO:
In this assignment, you are required to demonstrate your understanding of data science methodologies. Based on the requirements, this project aims to develop a user-friendly website.

GOAL:
This assignment integrates technical knowledge with practical web development skills through digital media.

PHASES OF THE CRISP-DM

  1. Business Understanding
    Design and develop a website of the methodology choosen based on the requirements given and presenting the website.

  2. Data Understanding
    Make a few online research on the Google to find out about the CRISP-DM. Based the references, try to understand the methodology.

  3. Data Preparation
    After collecting information about CRISP-DM, summarized and rewritten the information to ensure clarity, accurancy, and suitability to put inside the website.

  4. Modelling
    Design the website structure and navigation. For example, if want to make multi-page website architecture, a navigation menu and in-page links.

  5. Evalualtion
    Test the website by clicking all pages, links, images, and make sure the navigation function correctly.

  6. Deployment
    Preparing to submit this assignment and presenting it to the lecture.
